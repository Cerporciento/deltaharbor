Delta Harbor LLC

A remote-first digital asset company focused on cryptocurrency trading, portfolio management, and decentralized finance strategies. All operations are conducted globally through blockchain infrastructure and digital platforms.

About

Delta Harbor LLC is a U.S.-registered company designed to structure and manage professional activities in digital asset markets.

The company operates independently and remotely, without reliance on local clients or physical infrastructure, allowing full flexibility and global operation.

Founded by Francisco Serrano Korolov, the company focuses on disciplined trading systems, risk management, and long-term capital growth.
